What is Trade Theory?

Trade Theory explains the "Why" behind the practical work. It covers the technical calculations, scientific laws, and safety regulations essential for a professional.

  • 🛡️
    Occupational Safety Workplace safety rules, hazard prevention, and first-aid procedures to ensure a secure working environment.
  • 🛠️
    Tools & Equipment Detailed study of precision tools, their manufacturing materials, and the correct calibration methods.
  • 📐
    Core Engineering Laws The underlying mathematical and physical principles that govern your specific trade operations.

COPA Theory

Computer Operator & Programming Assistant logic, hardware architecture, and software rules.

  • 🖥️
    Computer Architecture In-depth study of CPU processing, Memory hierarchy (RAM/ROM), and Motherboard layout.
  • ⚙️
    OS & File Systems Booting mechanisms, Kernel operations, and differences between FAT32, NTFS, and ext4.
  • 🌐
    Networking Principles Understanding the 7-Layer OSI Model, IP addressing structures, and network topologies.
  • 🗄️
    Database Logic Relational Database theories, Primary vs. Foreign keys, and data normalization.

Electrician Theory

The physics of electrical flow, circuitry rules, and electromagnetic machine properties.

  • 🔌
    Electrical Physics Calculations involving Ohm's Law, Kirchhoff's current/voltage laws, and total power formulas.
  • 🔄
    Machine Principles Faraday's laws of electromagnetic induction driving both AC and DC motors/generators.
  • 🔋
    Transformer Mechanics Voltage stepping logic, magnetic flux paths, and understanding copper vs. core losses.
  • 🏗️
    Standard Wiring Laws Indian Electricity (I.E.) Rules for domestic wiring, and proper plate/pipe earthing techniques.

Electronics Theory

Behavior of electrons within semiconductor materials and micro-circuit engineering.

  • ⚛️
    Semiconductor Physics Atomic-level study of P-Type/N-Type doping and the creation of P-N depletion layers.
  • 📟
    Component Analysis How diodes rectify AC to DC, and the amplification/switching characteristics of BJT and FET transistors.
  • 🔢
    Digital Logic Design Binary and Hexadecimal mathematics, Boolean algebra, and standard Logic Gate truth tables.
  • 📡
    Signal Communication Theoretical concepts of Amplitude (AM) and Frequency (FM) modulation and transmission.
